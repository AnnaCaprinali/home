About me

PhD candidate in Sociology and Social Research at University of Trento

Research interests

My research work entails gender studies and demography, using an interdisciplinary approach to investigate potential inequalities resulting from the interaction of gender and sexual orientation in various aspects of life. Recently, my research has focused on the life trajectories of non-heterosexual individuals and how sexual orientation influences aspects such as partnership transitions and educational careers. My ongoing PhD project explores the extent to which sexual orientation is related to and affects educational attainment, and how it contributes to shape adult opportunities for LGB(TQIA*) pupils.
